The Frenchmen

You must attack the castle belonging to Guy de Loimbard. His French Guards say that they have the Holy Grail (it's very nice) and they refuse to give it to you.
The French guard will choose a random letter from the first half of the alphabet. You will have 5 guesses to get it right. If you get the guesses wrong, then fetchez la vache.
If the French fetchez la vache, then the guard will choose another random letter from the second half of the alphabet. If you do not get it right in 5 tries, well, you will see.
Each time you get an answer right, the French guard will choose another letter. Get 3 guesses right to obtain the Holy Hand Grenade of Antioch,
which God hath granteth that thoust may smite thy foes.
And be aware! The French are sneaky and may choose the same letter again!
